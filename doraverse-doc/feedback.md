# Feedback

There are two ways to send feedback to Doraverse:

## **In your workspace:**

1. Click on **Admin Settings** in **Account** at bottom-left corner of the screen.
2. Select **Feedback**.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/Feedback (1).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

An in-app feedback widget will appear on your screen, right inside your workspace.

## **Via direct link**

Visit [forms.doraverse.com/feedback](https://forms.doraverse.com/feedback) to share ideas or issues and help us build a smarter, more efficient AI coworker for you.
